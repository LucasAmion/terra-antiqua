TerraAntiqua

A QGIS plugin

The plugin creates a paleoDEM by combining present day topography and paleobathimetry, and modiying the final topography by introducing masks. Generated by Plugin Builder: http://g-sherman.github.io/Qgis-Plugin-Builder/
